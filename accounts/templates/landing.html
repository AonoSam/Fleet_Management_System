About Us

Built for fleet operators
in Kenya

Tronilla Fleet Management Saccoh is a Kisumu-based transport cooperative dedicated to empowering fleet operators with modern digital tools. We understand the unique challenges of running a fleet in Kenya — from managing driver payments to tracking vehicle health.

Our system is built to eliminate paperwork, reduce errors, and give every stakeholder — from admin to driver — a clear view of what's happening across the fleet.
